About Us

Our Mission

Afro-Unity Sports Foundation empowers communities across Uganda through inclusive sports programs that promote unity, health, gender equality, and youth development. We are dedicated to using sports as a catalyst for positive change, ensuring that every individual, regardless of gender, has the opportunity to thrive and contribute to the community.

Our Vision

A united and equitable Uganda where sports serve as a powerful tool for social change, fostering gender balance, youth empowerment, and community resilience. We strive to lead the way in creating inclusive environments where everyone especiall the youth, can realize their full potential

Our Goals

Expand Access to Inclusive Sports

Increase participation in sports activities across Uganda, with a focus on mixed-gender sports like Baseball5.

Promote Health and Wellness

Collaborate with partners to integrate health awareness into our sports programs to improve community health outcomes.

Foster Youth Development and Gender Equality

Provide opportunities for young people of all genders to develop life skills, leadership qualities, and educational achievements.

Build Strong, Gender-Inclusive Communities

Use sports as a tool to foster unity, social cohesion, and resilience, ensuring equal contributions from all genders.

Sustainability and Growth

Secure partnerships, sponsorships, and donations to ensure long-term sustainability and expansion of our programs.
