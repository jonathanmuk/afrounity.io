Our Mission

Afro-Unity Sports Foundation empowers communities across Uganda through inclusive sports programs that promote unity, health, gender equality, and youth development.

A united and equitable Uganda where sports serve as a powerful tool for social change, fostering gender balance, youth empowerment, and community resilience.

Our Strategic Goals

Expand Access

Increase participation in sports activities across Uganda.

Promote Health

Integrate health awareness into our sports programs.

Foster Youth Development

Provide opportunities for young people to develop life skills.

Build Inclusive Communities

Use sports as a tool to foster unity and social cohesion.

Ensure Sustainability

Secure partnerships for long-term program sustainability.
